Overview

We collect 2,198 soccer video clips from 2018 World Cup with match commentary from Hupu forum. Each clip is annotated across multiple categories, totaling 12 distinct classes. 1,458 clips are designated for training and 740 for testing. We employ Mask R-CNN to detect persons for detector-based GAR methods and extract C3D features video captioning. The train-test split of this dataset is performed at video level, rather than at frame level.

Download links: Baidu Cloud or Google Drive. Limited by the copyright, this dataset is available upon request and only for academic research. Please contact me by email (peidx _at_ buaa.edu.cn).

Structure

        .\                                                             ......Path of FIFAWC
        |--- srcs                                                      ......Original file path
                |--- 6-14-Russia-Saudi-5-0                             ......Parent path for a single match
                |--- ├── 0.jpg                                      ......"*.jpg" usually represents an overview of the entire match
                |--- ├── 1.gif                                      ......"*.gif" represents different video clips of the match
                |--- ├── gif-record.txt                             ......"gif-record.txt" contains the original Chinese captions of different video clips
                |--- |--- 1                                            ......Path corresponding to 1.gif
                |--- |--- ├── 000000.jpg                            ......Frame 0 of 1.gif (in jpg format)
                |--- |--- ├── 000000.png                            ......Frame 0 of 1.gif (in png format)
                |--- |--- ├── 000001.fc6-1                          ......C3D features (fc6 layer) of Frame 0 and the next 15 frames of 1.gif
                |--- |--- ├── 000001.fc7-1                          ......C3D features (fc7 layer) of Frame 0 and the next 15 frames of 1.gif
                |--- |--- ├── 000001.prob                           ......C3D outputs of Frame 0 and the next 15 frames of 1.gif
        |--- captions_new                                              ......Path for English captions
                ├── 6-14-Russia-Saudi-5-0.json                      ......Contains English captions of all clips for the corresponding match
        |--- csvs_new                                                  ......Path for multi-group activity categories
                ├── 6-14-Russia-Saudi-5-0.csv                       ......Contains multi-group activity categories for all clips of the corresponding match
        |--- fwc_det                                                   ......Path for human detection boxes in images
                |--- 6-14-Russia-Saudi-5-0                             ......Path for the corresponding match
                |--- ├── 1.txt                                      ......Detection results for video clips in the corresponding match
        ├── test_num.txt                                            ......List of test samples
        ├── train_num.txt                                           ......List of training samples
